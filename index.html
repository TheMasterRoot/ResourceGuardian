Control what pages load

Instantly see all first‑ and third‑party hosts a page loads and block what you don’t want with one click. No scripts or advanced setup needed. Private by design.

  • Live host discovery grouped by base domain
  • One‑click block/unblock for domains and specific URLs (Manifest V3 + DNR)
  • Per‑URL preview with open/copy tools
  • Search, filters, sorting, and bulk actions
  • Export/import your block lists
  • Optional risk checks using URLhaus (malware) and StevenBlack; third‑party auto‑block
  • Internationalized UI with many languages and RTL support
  • No data collection; all processing is local

Permissions, briefly

  • Read and change data on all sites: to list and block the requests you choose
  • Declarative Net Request: to install dynamic blocking rules
  • WebRequest (passive): to measure and display requests (does not intercept traffic)
  • Storage: to save your local settings and lists

Privacy

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data. All analysis happens locally on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
